<h1 id="pagetitle">Hospital Bed Capacity </h1>






<p>The bed occupancy data below comes from Hospital Capacity Direct Access (HCDA) data collection. Hospitals are required to report the bed counts every two hours and the data reflect the number of hospital and ICU beds available as reported by hospitals through HCDA 2PM of each day.</p>
<p>This dashboard will be updated after 3PM from Monday through Friday except holidays.</p>
